Rolling Car Show - Burrows Park

Sunday June 27th 2021 - 10AM

Meet other car enthusiasts and bring any vehicle you chose to the Rolling Car Show. While Sports and Clasic Vehicles are encouraged, they and vehicles of any kind are not a requirement. This event is open to all people of all ages, with the only prerequisite is an interest in moving vehicles. After about an hour of meeting and talking about our shared interests, at approxiamtely 11am, there will be a short parade through the village obeying all posted speed limits (15mph) followed by a short 'country' drive in the outlying Madison area. Please join us and share in our passion.
